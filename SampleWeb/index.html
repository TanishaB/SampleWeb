India is a land of diversified culture and varied customs. It is a country of many different people, culture, customs , cusines and sweets. Yes, that’s correct, “Sweets” popularly known as Mithai in India.
Indian sweets are a type of confectionery that rely heavily on sugar, milk, flour and condensed milk. Although the bases of these sweets vary among regions, one thing is probably universal throughout the length and breadth of this vast country, it is the love for these colourful sweets which every Indian shares equally.
